# 📦 Product Analytics

This analysis evaluates product performance, margin behavior, price categories, and return rates across subcategories to optimize inventory health.

---

## 💻 Product Analytics View

![Product Analytics](Product.png)

### 📈 Core Business Drivers
* **Gross Margin (GM%):** Maintained an exceptionally strong baseline at **68.7%**.
  
* **Cost Structure (COST%):** Tight procurement controls kept total costs at **31.32%**.
  
* **Return Impact (%Returns.Amount):** Financial impact from returns is restricted to **8.11%**.
  
* **Return Frequency (%Returns.Orders):** Return volume is high, affecting **40.73%** of total system orders.

---

## 🔄 Product Performance Breakdowns

### 1️⃣ Revenue & Profit by Price Category
* **0-1000 Tier:** Driving the highest volume with **$36M** in Revenue and **$24M** in Profit.
  
* **1000-2000 Tier:** Solid mid-range output securing **$32M** in Revenue and **$22M** in Profit.
  
* **>2000 Tier:** Premium baseline capturing **$11M** in Revenue and **$8M** in Profit.

### 2️⃣ Cost & GM% Alignment
* **Volume Cost Burn:** The 0-1000 category absorbs the highest cost outlay at **$11.6M** with a **67.8%** margin.
  
* **Margin Optimization:** The premium >2000 tier unlocks the highest profitability rate, peaking at **71.3%** Gross Margin.

### 3️⃣ High-Risk Return Subcategories (Order Volume)
* **Whey Protein:** The absolute highest return volume node with **441 returned orders**.
  
* **Egg Protein:** Follows closely as a major operational friction point with **373 returned orders**.
  
* **Meal Replacement:** Third major risk anchor generating **239 returned orders**.

---

## 🔍 Analytical Takeaway

**Maximum profitability is achieved by balancing volume-driven categories with strict return-prevention controls:**

* **🚀 The Volume Engine (0-1000 Tier):** Anchors total business scale, single-handedly generating the largest share of revenue (**$36M**) and profit (**$24M**), proving that low-to-mid tier pricing dictates corporate market share.
  
* **⚠️ The Operational Risk (Protein Returns):** While the *Protein* family drives massive revenue, it represents the primary point of logistical failure, with *Whey Protein* (**441**) and *Egg Protein* (**373**) topping the return charts and inflating the overall order return rate to **40.73%**.

**💡 Strategic Product Rhythm:** Protect high-volume revenue by rolling out targeted quality audits or packaging re-alignments specifically for the *Whey* and *Egg Protein* catalogs to suppress the **40.73%** return rate, while aggressively expanding inventory for premium **>2000** tier products to capture high-velocity **71.3%** gross margins.
