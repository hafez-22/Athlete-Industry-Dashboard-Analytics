# Athlete Industry Analytics Dashboard



## Business Context



As the athlete industry expanded across multiple product categories, customer segments, and regional markets, tracking business performance became increasingly complex. Traditional reporting provided isolated metrics but lacked visibility into the drivers behind profitability changes, return behavior, customer retention, and regional performance differences.



This project was developed as an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards, data modeling, and analytical reporting.



---



## Project Objectives



The analysis was designed to answer key business questions:



* Which product categories drive the highest revenue and profitability?

* How do pricing tiers affect margin performance?

* Which customer segments show the strongest retention behavior?

* Where is revenue leakage occurring across return operations?

* Which regions contribute most to growth and operational pressure?

* How do seasonal cost fluctuations impact profitability over time?

### 5. Returns Analytics

## Analytical Workflow



The project followed a structured BI workflow:



1. Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query.

2. Building the relational data model (Star Schema).

3. Creating calculated KPIs and DAX measures.

4. Designing interactive, user-centric dashboards.

5. Extracting insights and translating findings into business recommendations.



---



## Executive Insights



* **Revenue & Margins:** Revenue growth remained strong across the analyzed period, supported by stable margins and increasing sales volume.

* **Product Dependency:** The business showed a heavy dependency on protein-related products, making category performance highly influential on overall profitability.

* **Return Operations:** Return operations exposed a major source of revenue leakage, particularly across high-volume supplement products.

* **Retention Metrics:** Customer behavior analysis revealed a stable repeat-purchase segment alongside inactive accounts that may require targeted retention strategies.

* **Cost Fluctuations:** Seasonal operational cost spikes created short-term pressure on profitability despite long-term revenue growth.
