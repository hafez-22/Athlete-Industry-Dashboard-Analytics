# 📊 Athlete Industry Analytics Dashboard

---

## Business Context

As the Athlete Industry expanded across multiple product categories, customer segments, and regional markets, monitoring business performance became increasingly complex.

Traditional reporting methods provided isolated metrics, but failed to explain the operational drivers behind profitability fluctuations, return activity, customer retention, and regional performance differences.

To address these challenges, this project was developed as a centralized Business Intelligence solution designed to transform raw transactional data into actionable business insights through interactive analytics and structured data modeling.

---

## Project Objectives

The analysis focused on answering several critical business questions:

- Which product categories generate the strongest revenue and profitability?
- How do pricing tiers impact margin performance?
- Which customers represent the most stable retention groups?
- Where is revenue leakage occurring across return operations?
- Which regions are driving growth and operational pressure?
- How do seasonal cost fluctuations affect profitability over time?

## Analytical Approach

The project followed a structured analytical workflow that included:

1. Data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
   
2. Building a relational data model
 
3. Creating calculated measures and KPIs using DAX

4. Designing interactive dashboards for business analysis
  
5. Extracting insights and translating findings into business recommendations

## Key Findings

The analysis revealed that products priced below **$1,000** represented the company’s strongest commercial segment, generating nearly **$36M in revenue** and approximately **$24M in net profit**.

Customer purchasing behavior showed a stable retention pattern among repeat buyers, while **66 inactive customers** were identified as potential churn risks requiring further engagement strategies.

Revenue performance demonstrated steady long-term growth, reaching approximately **$60.59M** across the analyzed period.

Return analysis uncovered nearly **$6.39M in lost value** linked to damaged and rejected orders, exposing a significant source of operational and profitability pressure.

Regional analysis identified the **Southwest market** as the company’s leading profit and customer acquisition region, while simultaneously recording the highest return activity.

---

## Strategic Insights

One of the most important findings was that higher margin percentages did not necessarily translate into stronger profitability.

Although premium products priced above **$2,000** achieved the highest gross margin rate (**71.3%**), mid-range products consistently delivered stronger overall profit due to significantly higher sales volume.

The analysis also highlighted supplement products as the largest contributor to return-related losses, particularly the **100% Egg Protein**, **Pure Casein**, and **Hydrolyzed Whey** product lines.

Additionally, monthly operational costs showed noticeable spikes during **February** and **October**, placing short-term pressure on profitability despite stable annual revenue growth.

---

## Executive Summary

The analysis demonstrated that the business maintains strong overall financial performance supported by healthy revenue growth and margin stability. However, profitability remains highly sensitive to return activity, operational inefficiencies, and seasonal cost fluctuations.

The dashboard enabled management to identify major revenue drivers, monitor regional operational issues, and isolate key sources of revenue leakage across products and fulfillment operations.

Overall, the project provides a centralized analytical solution that supports faster, more accurate, and data-driven decision-making across sales, operations, customer performance, and profitability management.
